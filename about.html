About

Backend depth, mobile range, AI curiosity, and product sense.

My engineering path started with strong fundamentals: Java, Python, MySQL, object-oriented design, and structured backend projects. That foundation helped me understand how software behaves beyond the screen: data flow, validation, persistence, routing, and the everyday discipline of making systems predictable.

I have built across Django, Flutter, Electron, Java, Python, JavaScript, and database-backed systems. These days I spend a lot of time with React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Flutter, and React Native because I like building complete products: the interface, the API, the data model, the mobile experience, and the small details that make software feel trustworthy.

I train and experiment with machine learning models from time to time, and I am a strong enthusiast of AI technology. I believe that in the modern era it is necessary to adapt to AI while staying connected to roots and fundamentals. AI tools can speed up research, exploration, and execution, but I do not let them blindly drive the work. Because I understand the basics, I know when to use AI, what to instruct, what to verify, and when to rely on engineering judgment.

I like thinking from a business standpoint: what problem is being solved, what workflow becomes easier, and what risks the product must avoid. I care about writing clean, secure code, designing reliable APIs, and reducing vulnerabilities instead of treating security as an afterthought.
